BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a measure of body fat based on a person's weight and height. It is commonly used to determine if a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. BMI can help individuals assess their overall health and potential risks for diseases associated with excess weight

How can I determine if my weight is within a healthy range as an adult aged 18 years or older?

BMI < 18.5

This is the interpretation of a BMI value less than 18.5. It means that the person is underweight and should engage in more physical activity, have a balanced diet, and increase overall fitness to maintain good health.

18.5 ≤ BMI < 24

Congratulations! You have a "healthy weight" range with a BMI between 18.5 and 24. Keep up the good work!

24 ≤ BMI < 27

You are considered to be overweight, and it is recommended that you take action to manage your weight to a healthy level."

BMI ≥ 27
